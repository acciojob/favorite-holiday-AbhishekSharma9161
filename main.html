Maldives


The Maldives is a tropical paradise known for its crystal-clear waters, luxurious overwater villas, and serene white-sand beaches. It is one of the most popular destinations for relaxation, honeymoons, and water adventures.

  1. Snorkeling in the vibrant coral reefs
  2. Visiting the Bioluminescent Beach at Vaadhoo Island
  3. Staying in a luxurious overwater villa
  4. Exploring the Male Local Market
  5. Taking a sunset cruise across the Indian Ocean
  • Mas Huni – a traditional Maldivian breakfast dish
  • Garudhiya – a flavorful fish soup
  • Fihunu Mas – grilled and spiced fish
  • Saagu Bondibai – a sweet sago dessert
  • Bis Keemiya – a crispy snack similar to samosa
