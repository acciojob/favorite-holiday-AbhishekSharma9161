Paris


Paris, the capital of France, is known for its rich history, iconic landmarks, beautiful architecture, and world-class art museums. It is one of the most popular travel destinations in the world.

  1. The Eiffel Tower, the most iconic symbol of Paris
  2. The Louvre Museum with world-famous artworks
  3. A walk along the Champs-Élysées
  4. Exploring the historic Notre-Dame Cathedral
  5. A cruise on the Seine River
  • Croissants – buttery and flaky French pastry
  • Macarons – colorful sweet confection
  • Baguette – crispy and classic French bread
  • Crème Brûlée – creamy dessert with caramelized top
  • Ratatouille – traditional vegetable dish
