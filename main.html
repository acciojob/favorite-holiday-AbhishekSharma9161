Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is often called the "City of Light." It's known for its art, culture, romantic ambiance, and iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and Louvre Museum.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – the most famous landmark and symbol of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – home to the world-renowned Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a beautiful example of Gothic architecture.
  4. Seine River Cruise – a romantic way to view the illuminated city at night.
  5. Montmartre – known for its artistic history and stunning city views.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – soft, buttery, and flaky pastries.
  • Crêpes – thin pancakes filled with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Macarons – colorful, delicate cookies with creamy centers.
  • French Onion Soup – rich soup topped with cheese and bread.
  • Escargot – snails cooked in garlic butter, a classic French dish.
