Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is known as the “City of Light.” It is famous for its stunning architecture, world-class museums, romantic atmosphere, and exquisite cuisine. Whether you're walking along the Seine or visiting its historic landmarks, Paris offers something special for everyone.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – the iconic symbol of Paris and a must-see attraction.
  2. Louvre Museum – home to the world-famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – a lively avenue filled with shops, cafés, and theaters.
  5. Seine River Cruise – offers a romantic view of illuminated Paris at night.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – buttery and flaky, perfect for breakfast.
  • Crêpes – thin pancakes filled with sweet or savory ingredients.
  • French Onion Soup – rich and flavorful, topped with melted cheese.
  • Macarons – colorful almond cookies with soft, creamy fillings.
  • Escargot – snails cooked in garlic butter, a classic French delicacy.
